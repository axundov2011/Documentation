Manage Seller

This collection of articles guides you through how to manage your Seller Request, Withdraw Request & other components.

Registration for a seller​

A seller can registration his/her shop by sign up from website. Then if admin approved the seller then he/she can sign in the seller admin panel and configure the seller panel for business.

Seller can sell his/her product by adding the product from his admin panel also update his/her shop information ,can add bank account , Seller can also add shipping method and delivery man if admin gives the permission and also can contact with the customer throw the message service. From the Withdraws menu they can see the payment list what they will get from the admin.

Seller can see the order list and order details and manage their order from Order Management section.
